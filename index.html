    About the Wari

    माउली

    Each year, lakhs of Warkaris walk the palkhi of Sant Dnyaneshwar Maharaj from Alandi and Sant Tukaram Maharaj from Dehu toward the Vitthal Rukmini temple in Pandharpur, timing their arrival for Ashadhi Ekadashi.

    Pune Police runs the official live tracker to help both devotees and daily commuters navigate the city safely while the processions pass through — this page gives that same feed a calmer home, with the traffic and schedule context around it.
